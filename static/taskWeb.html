Your Web Task Mission!


Description

Produce a webpage with your details on. Details could include:

  • Introduction of yourself
  • Hobbies
  • An interesting fact
  • Your goals and where you see yourself in the future

Feel free to add anything else onto this page. We want you to be as creative as possible with the elements (e.g.forms,maps,images). As well as incorporating loads of elements, you need to make your page stand out and look attractive.


If you need some help getting started, check out this website: https://www.w3schools.com/ Also reach out to your peers for help, you can all learn cool and different things from each other and this is one of the key aspects we adopt at BlackRock.

You will be asked to demo your page on the final day, so make sure it is cool and exciting. Go wild!

Goodluck and lets get coding!
