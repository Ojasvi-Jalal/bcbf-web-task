Welcome to BlackRock!

Firstly, welcome to BlackRock. We want you to feel like you are part of us, so be active, ask loads of questions, get involved and most importantly, HAVE FUN!

The Agenda

For the 9 days you are here, you will take part in various activities such as:

  • Social Events
  • Creating your own website

This will be an action packed event, but we want you to do as much as possible and show us and yourself what you are capable of! Don't forget to take breaks whenever you feel and reach out to anyone on the floor to point you in the right direction, we are all here to help!
